Scientific Methodology & Valuation Framework

I. Biomass & DBH Conversion

Circumference (C) is measured in the field and converted to Diameter at Breast Height (d): d = C / π Biomass is estimated using regressions from Jenkins et al. (2003) where dbh_cm = d × 2.54.

II. Sequestration & Economic Cost

CO2 Equivalent is derived using a molecular weight ratio of 3.6663. Valuation follows the U.S. EPA (2023) Social Cost of Carbon central estimate of $210 per metric ton.
