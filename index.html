Scientific Methodology

I. Field-to-Lab Conversion

To ensure accessibility for citizen scientists, students measure circumference (C). The system automatically derives Diameter at Breast Height (DBH) using the geometric constant $\pi$:

$d = C / \pi$

Biomass ($bm$) is then calculated using species-specific regressions from Jenkins et al. (2003) where $dbh_{cm} = d \times 2.54$.

II. Economic Valuation

Valuation is based on the Social Cost of Carbon (U.S. EPA, 2023), currently modeled at $210 per metric ton.
