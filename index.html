Scientific Methodology & Citations

I. Biomass Regression Models

Calculations utilize total above-ground biomass (AGB) equations from Jenkins, J.C., et al. (2003) "Comprehensive database of diameter-based regression equations for North American tree species."

Biomass (kg) = Exp(b0 + b1 * ln(DBH_cm))

II. Carbon & Economic Indexing

Carbon Density: Dry tree biomass is assumed to be 50% elemental carbon. This is converted to CO2 equivalents by the molecular ratio of CO2 to C (3.6663).

Valuation: Indexed to the U.S. EPA (2023) Social Cost of Carbon (~$0.09 per lb CO2e).
