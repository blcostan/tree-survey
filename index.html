Methodology & Valuation Brief

Biomass Estimation: This tool utilizes the Jenkins et al. (2003) national-scale biomass regressions. Calculations use the generalized logarithmic equation: bm = exp(b0 + b1 * ln(dbh)), where bm is total aboveground biomass in kilograms and dbh is diameter at breast height in centimeters.

Carbon Fraction: Total biomass is converted to carbon (C) using a standard 0.5 dry-weight fraction, and subsequently to CO2 equivalent using the 3.6663 molecular weight ratio (44/12).

Economic Valuation: The Ecosystem Asset Value reflects the Social Cost of Carbon (SCC), representing the avoided global economic damages (climate, health, agriculture) per metric ton of CO2 removed. Per the EPA's 2023 climate review, the 2026 central estimate is valued at $210/metric ton.
