Scientific Methodology & Citations

I. Annual Increment Modeling

Calculations utilize total above-ground biomass (AGB) equations from Jenkins, J.C., et al. (2003). Annual sequestration is derived via the Current Annual Increment (CAI) method, assuming a mean radial growth of 0.2"/year adjusted by health factor.

II. Carbon & Economic Indexing

Molecular Ratio: Biomass is ~50% carbon; CO2e utilizes the 3.6663 atomic weight ratio.

Valuation: Calculated via EPA Social Cost of Carbon (~$190/metric ton or $0.09/lb CO2e).
