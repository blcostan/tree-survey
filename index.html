Scientific Methodology & Carbon Logic

I. The Biomass Equation

We use the Jenkins Allometric Power Law to estimate total tree weight based on Diameter at Breast Height (DBH).

Mass = exp(Constant_A + Constant_B * ln(DBH))

II. Carbon Fraction

Approximately 50% of a tree's dry mass is pure Carbon, physically sequestered from the atmosphere.

III. Atmosphere to Wood

To find total $CO_2$ removed, we multiply the Carbon weight by 3.666, representing the molecular ratio of $CO_2$ to $C$.

Final Formula Logic:

[(Biomass × 0.5) × 3.666] × Health Modifier = Total $CO_2$ Saved
