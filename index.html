Scientific Methodology & Citations

I. Lifetime Storage Modeling

Calculations utilize total above-ground biomass (AGB) equations from Jenkins, J.C., et al. (2003). This registry calculates the Total Carbon Bank, representing the cumulative $CO_2$ sequestered and stored over the tree's entire lifespan to date.

II. Carbon & Economic Indexing

Economic Valuation: Social value represents the Total Asset Value of the stored carbon based on the EPA Social Cost of Carbon ($190/metric ton), or $0.09 per pound of CO2e banked.
