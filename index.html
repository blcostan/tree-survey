Scientific Methodology & Citations

I. Biomass Models

Based on Jenkins et al. (2003) parameters. Biomass is estimated from DBH in centimeters using species-specific coefficients.

BM = Exp(b0 + b1 * ln(DBH_cm))

II. Carbon Valuation

Carbon is 50% of biomass. Value is indexed to the U.S. EPA (2023) Social Cost of Carbon (~$0.09/lb CO2e).
